Privacy Policy


Thank you for using Image to SVG! This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

Data Collection

We collect the following types of data from you:

  • Images: We require access to your device's camera or photo library to convert images to SVG format. We do not store any images that you convert.

  • User Data: We may collect usage data, such as the number of images you have converted, in order to improve our app's functionality and user experience. We do not collect any personal information, such as your name or email address.
